#HIGHGIRLCOMMUNITY

Our High Girl Community launched in January, and we've been the dopest digital things with our members. Our exclusive community exists to connect High Girl's through digital activations, IRL events, and thought provoking conversations. Here's a few things we did last month:

Q&A WITH JASMINE MANS

Our Roll Up & Read book club is held in the HGC, and the first book we read was Black Girl Call Home by Jasmine Mans. After a month of sharing and reflecting about the book in the group, we had an exclusive Q&A with the poet and owner of Buy Weed From Women.

*The full conversation is available with membership*

Q&A WITH JASMINE MANS

We hosted an exclusive [virtual & remote] taste test & first look at Elixi, a new BlackWoman-owned cannabis company, before it hits the market!

Q&A WITH WHITNEY BEAUTY

We hosted our very first Takeover After-Party with Founder of Josephine & Billie's, the first dispensary dedicated to women of color. Whitney delivered knowledge on how to navigate the cannabis industry as a Black Woman, and some of the struggles and triumphs she has experienced.

*The full conversation is available with membership*

CANDLE CREATING WORKSHOP

A good candle is a great weapon of choice for smudging out the weed smell. Ani & Co. hosted an intimate candle making workshop & smoke sesh with the HGC .
